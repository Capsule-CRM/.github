## Fixes for Common Adoption Gaps

If a Capsule CRM login does not show expected records, check workspace permissions, filters, ownership settings, and whether the import was completed under the correct account. Many early issues come from hidden saved lists or records assigned to another user.

If Capsule CRM email integration is incomplete, reconnect the mailbox, confirm forwarding or sync settings, and test with a single contact before relying on full history capture. For Capsule CRM integrations such as Capsule CRM Xero integration, verify matching customer names and account permissions before troubleshooting deeper.

If the team is comparing Capsule CRM alternatives because adoption feels slow, simplify the first pipeline, remove unused custom fields, and focus on the few Capsule CRM features that support daily follow-up. A short Capsule CRM demo using real contacts and real opportunities often helps users see the value faster than a generic walkthrough.
